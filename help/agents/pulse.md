# Pulse Agent

Pulse is the unified notification service in Adobe CX Enterprise. The Pulse Agent adds a conversational AI layer within [AI Assistant](../ai-assistant/ai-assistant-ui.md) that you can use to summarize, interpret, and act on your notifications with natural language, thereby turning a passive feed into an active assistant. Instead of scanning a long list, you can go from "I have 47 notifications" to "2 need my approval by Friday, 3 datasets have conflicts, the rest is FYI" in a single exchange.

## What Pulse Agent can do

Pulse Agent supports the following capabilities:

| Capabilities | Description |
| --- | --- |
| **Notification summaries and insights** | Get concise overviews of your notifications, clearly distinguish between those that require action and those that are just informational, and understand both the purpose of a notification and the reason you received it. |
| **Operational actions** | Take action on notifications directly through conversation—mark them as read, snooze them, or set reminders for individual or grouped notifications. |
| **Preferences and settings** | Enable or disable notification channels (email, Slack, in-app) per solution and category, without navigating Settings. |
| **Navigation and deep linking** | Jump from a notification directly to the relevant product area via deep links. |
| **Collaboration and sharing** | Share notifications or send announcements to specific users or the entire organization (authorized users) |

## Access Pulse Agent

You can access Pulse Agent in the following ways:

- **From AI Assistant**: Open AI Assistant and enter any notification-related prompt.
- **From email or Slack**: Select **[!UICONTROL Review with AI Assistan]t** on a notification to open AI Assistant with that notification's full context already loaded.

## Sample prompts

The following examples demonstrate prompts you can use with Pulse Agent.

### Summaries and insights

- "Summarize my recent notifications."
- "Which of my notifications need my action versus what's just FYI?"
- "Tell me more about this notification — why did I receive it?"

### Actions

- "Mark all non-actionable notifications as read."
- "Snooze all deployment-related notifications until tomorrow."
- "Remind me about the APAC Winback Campaign notification tomorrow morning."

### Preferences

- "Enable Slack notifications for Journey Optimizer."
- "Switch my daily digest to weekly."

### Navigation and sharing

- "Take me to where this notification is about."
- "Share this notification with my team."
- "Send an announcement about today's maintenance to the whole org."

## Best practices

Keep the following tips in mind when using Pulse Agent:

- **Be specific** — Name the app, category, or notification so the agent acts on the right target.
- **Chain prompts** — Ask for a summary, drill into one notification, then act on it, all in one conversation.
- **Answer clarifying questions** — Confirming the app, channel, or notification ensures changes apply correctly.
