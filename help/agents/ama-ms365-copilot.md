# Adobe Marketing Agent for [!DNL Microsoft 365 Copilot]

<!--OVERVIEW-->

Adobe Marketing Agent for [!DNL Microsoft 365 Copilot] is an AI agent that connects Adobe Experience Platform to [!DNL Microsoft 365 Copilot]. You can use this agent to ask natural-language questions in [!DNL Microsoft 365] surfaces such as [!DNL Teams] abd [!DNL Copilot], and retrieve marketing insights from Experience Platform without leaving your workflow.

With the Adobe Marketing Agent for [!DNL Microsoft 365 Copilot], marketing managers, analytics and insights teams, and business stakeholders can:

- Make faster, data-driven marketing decisions
- Reduce time spent switching between tools
- Simplify access to audience and journey insights across teams

## What you can do with Adobe Marketing Agent

| Use case | Description |
| --- | --- |
| Unlock marketing insights in [!DNL Microsoft 365] | Use the agent on your [!DNL Microsoft 365] application to retrieve tables, charts, and summaries directly, by asking questions such as: <ul><li>"Show web visitors for my top 10 product pages last week."</li><li>"Which audiences grew the most over the last month?"</li></ul> |
| Analyze customer journeys and campaigns | You can review journey performance and drop‑offs, compare campaign performance over time, and understand which touchpoints drive conversions. |
| Empower non-experts with guided prompts | Use pre‑built "starter prompts" to explore audiences, campaigns, and web traffic. Take advantage of a natural‑language interface for easier learning of Experience Platform and Customer Journey Analytics concepts. |

## How the agent works

Integrated Adobe + Microsoft experience

- Adobe Marketing Agent appears as an agent in Microsoft 365 Copilot / Teams.
- Users sign in with their Adobe account and select the data environment (for example, sandbox, data view).

Powered by Adobe Experience Platform

- Connects to Adobe Experience Platform and associated apps (e.g., Real‑Time CDP, Journey Optimizer, Customer Journey Analytics).
- Uses Adobe's AI Assistant and agents to interpret questions and retrieve relevant insights.

Results surfaced in Copilot

- Returns answers as conversational text, tables, and visualizations.
- Supports follow‑up, "dig deeper" questions within the same Copilot chat.

## Get started

Prerequisites

- Microsoft 365 with access to Microsoft 365 Copilot / Teams
- Eligible Adobe Experience Platform products (for example, Real‑Time CDP, Journey Optimizer and/or Customer Journey Analytics)
- Appropriate licenses and permissions within your Adobe and Microsoft environments

Installation & setup (high level)

- Add Adobe Marketing Agent from the Microsoft Copilot / Microsoft 365 app catalog.
- Sign in with your Adobe credentials and connect to your organization.
- Choose the relevant data environment (such as sandbox or data view) when prompted.

First session checklist

- Confirm that you can sign in and see example prompts
- Run a simple audience or web‑traffic query to validate access
- Try sharing a result in a Teams channel

## Security, Privacy, and Responsible AI

Data handling and governance

- Uses existing controls and governance from Adobe Experience Platform and Microsoft 365.
- Customers maintain ownership and control of their data.

Responsible AI use

- Designed to provide read‑only insights and not change customer data.
- Users are encouraged to review generated summaries and analyses before acting on them.

Supported languages & scope

- Initial release: English‑language experience.
- Focused on read‑only insights, not creating or updating marketing assets or configurations.
